About Us

Track My Fit is a simple fitness tracking application created to help users track their daily activities and improve their health. This app is designed for beginners who want to start their fitness journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage people to stay active and healthy by providing an easy-to-use fitness tracking platform.

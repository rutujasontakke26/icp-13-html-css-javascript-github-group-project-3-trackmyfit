About Us

Track My Fit is a beginner-friendly fitness tracking website designed to help users understand their health better. Our platform focuses on simplicity, clarity, and ease of use so that anyone can start their fitness journey without confusion.

Whether you are a student, working professional, or fitness enthusiast, Track My Fit provides essential tools to track health data and stay motivated.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote a healthy lifestyle by offering simple fitness calculators and workout planning tools. We believe fitness should be accessible to everyone, regardless of experience level.

Track My Fit encourages consistency, awareness, and self-improvement through small daily actions that lead to long-term health benefits.

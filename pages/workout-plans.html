Workout Plans

Chest Workout

Bench Press / Push-ups: 4×8–10
Incline Dumbbell Press: 3×10–12
Chest Fly (DB/Cable): 3×12–15
Machine Press / Dips: 3×10–12
Finisher: Push-ups to failure (2 rounds)

Leg Workout

Barbell Squats / Goblet Squats: 4×8–10
Leg Press: 3×12–15 (slow & deep)
Romanian Deadlifts: 3×10–12
Walking Lunges: 3×20 steps
Standing Calf Raises: 4×15–20

Front Raises (Plate/DB): 3×12
Rear Delt Fly / Face Pulls: 3×15
